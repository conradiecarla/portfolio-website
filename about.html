My Story

I’m a UX/UI designer born in South Africa, raised in Canada, and based in the UK.

I owe my professional background to the film industry, having spent the last ten years delivering visual effects work for various Hollywood productions in Canada, France, and the UK.

Managing and collaborating with teams across borders has given me a deep understanding of the people-centric nature of the creative process, especially within a highly technical context, and ultimately led me to explore UX/UI design in my personal time. This I’ve learned to be true: when cultivating captivating visual experiences, there is always so much more at play than meets the eye.

I’m now seeking a position in which I can use my organisational and design skills to connect with users and deliver digital solutions that are not only visually pleasing, but effective and meaningful.
